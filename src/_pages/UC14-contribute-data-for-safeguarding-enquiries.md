## Summary

As the administrator of a social care CMS, I need to be able to contribute records that show which individuals we are (or have been) working with to a single view system, so that professionals in other organisations can rely on the responses they get from this system.


## Description

This use case is split out from use case 10 and 14 to summarise the requirements of organisations who hold information relevant for frontline professionals conducting safeguarding enquiries to be able to contribute data about which individuals they are working with to a single view system. This would enable frontline professionals to identify who else holds information about an individual. But to be useful, anyone querying it must have confidence that the responses they receive are based on:

* Responses from relevant organisations
* A comprehensive, reliable, and up to date picture of who each organisation has contact with

For wider context and details on the use case, please refer to use case 10.
